What ?

An election is a formal process where individuals in a community choose a person or persons to hold public office. It is a fundamental aspect of democratic societies, allowing citizens to participate in decision-making and shape the direction of their government.

Why ?

Voting empowers individuals to contribute to positive change in their communities and the broader society. By engaging in the democratic process, citizens can influence policies and decisions that impact their lives.
